Projects  –  Enmaven

Enmaven

ENmaven is a company for English teachers and schools all over the world working with children in preschool and early school age.

My role in this project was to understand target groups by research, design the entire process of getting to know the brand and process of buying products, design of the the website (through UX and UI design), create content on the website (copy) and plan communication strategy of a brand (such as: mission, vision, values, tone of voice, rand personality etc)

Problem Statement:

Challenges:

Easy to use platform for teachers all over the word that enables teacher to buy all kinds of material needed to conduct valuable lessons
A well thought-out brand communication strategy that responds to the hidden needs and problems of the target group

Main research goals

  • How to communicate with the target group? (who are the customers, what problems and difficulties they have, what kind of teachers they are, what are their motivations etc)
  • How to organize and present information on the platform? (what information do they need, how do they choose materials, what are they guided by when choosing materials, what is important for them at work etc)

Research analysis

The research showed that the first activity that teachers perform is searching for materials by keywords. Thanks to the search engine placed in the first viewport, we enable quick transition to the key action for users.

All information on the lesson tiles are the factors deciding on the choice / purchase of a given material, as declared by the respondents.

  • duration of the lesson
  • number of downloads and opinion
  • information about the type of English (British English / American English)
  • age of the students

Insights

There are few problems to solve when it comes to teaching as an English teacher.
First, teachers need to be smart and creative to attract their students and be sure that they'll be effective.
Second, they need to meet expectations of not only their students but also parents, which means that they need to be professional and their lessons needs to be practical.
Last but not least, there are also their own ambitions and needs like being THAT teacher everyone will remember.

Enmaven slogan

Useful. Fun. Memorable. The English lessons you always wanted.

Brand personality

Thanks to two overarching archetypes: Sage and Caregiver, I distinguished several features of the brand. ENmaven is smart, polite, genuine and always willing to help. ENmaven is like that one super clever friend you can talk to, laugh with, and count on for good advice.

Values

ENmaven is wise, fun, empathetic and inclusive.
